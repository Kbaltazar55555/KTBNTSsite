Values

In my last 10 years in SaaS, I developed a hunger for creativity and innovation - collaborating with others from different backgrounds energizes me to go faster, and develop better for those around me. With this however, I stay humble to not steamroll these conversations. I lead with a beginners mindset to achieve pragmatic solutions to business problems both internal and external. I am a champion for inclusivity, fostering a safe environment where myself and my colleagues feel valued and heard.

Goals

I was blessed with the opportunity to represent Tableau software in the Asia-Pacific region for several years, championing data governance and the importance analytics. My ultimate goal is to continue playing a role in the development process, both with my experience with Customer Success, Program Management, and Developer skill sets - i'd like to learn from a team that has that same innovative passion to transform their industry on a global scale.

Hobbies

Learning new langauges (programing and linguistic), stretching, reading, coffee, learning from new people, cooking.
